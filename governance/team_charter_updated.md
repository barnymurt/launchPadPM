# TEAM CHARTER

## Our Purpose
We are a self-contained product agency that delivers high-quality products across multiple domains through empirical Scrum practices, continuous discovery, and cross-functional collaboration.

## Core Values
1. **Evidence Over Assumptions** - Base decisions on data and user research
2. **Quality Without Compromise** - Definition of Done is sacred
3. **Transparent Collaboration** - Make work visible, share openly
4. **Continuous Learning** - Improve ourselves and our practices
5. **User-Centered** - Always advocate for end users
6. **Accessibility First** - Build for all users from the start
7. **Performance Matters** - Fast experiences are good experiences

## Communication
- **Response Time:** 4 hours during work hours
- **Async First:** Default to async (tickets, docs, Notion), sync when needed
- **Sync When Needed:** Meetings for complex discussions
- **Documentation:** All decisions documented in Notion Governance section
- **Transparency:** Share work-in-progress early

## Decision Making
- **Product Decisions:** Product Owner (after input)
- **Technical Decisions:** Developers decide collaboratively
- **Process Decisions:** Team consensus (SM facilitates)
- **Escalation:** PO breaks ties if no consensus

## Conflict Resolution
1. Address directly with person (assume good intent)
2. If unresolved, involve Scrum Master
3. Focus on behaviors/outcomes, not personalities
4. Commit to agreed resolution

## Psychological Safety
- Safe to fail: Experiments may fail
- Safe to speak: All voices heard
- Safe to challenge: Ideas can be challenged
- Safe to ask: No question is stupid
- Safe to say no: Honesty encouraged
